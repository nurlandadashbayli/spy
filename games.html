How to Play Scrabble

Objective: Score the most points by forming valid Azerbaijani words horizontally or vertically on the board.

First Turn: The first word played must cover the center star tile.

Connecting Words: All subsequent words must connect to at least one existing tile on the board.

Scoring: Each letter has a point value. The total score for a turn is the sum of the letters in all new words formed. Premium squares multiply the letter or word score:

  • 2x Letter (L2) / 3x Letter (L3)
  • 2x Word (W2) / 3x Word (W3)

Bingo: If you play all 7 tiles from your rack in one turn, you receive a 50-point bonus!

Special Tiles & Mechanics:

  • Blank Tile (*): Worth 0 points, but can represent any letter. You will be prompted to choose its letter when placed.
  • 🔄 Swap: Skip your turn to exchange any number of your tiles with the bag.
  • 🔀 Shuffle: Reorganize your rack (once per turn) to help spot words. This does NOT skip your turn.
