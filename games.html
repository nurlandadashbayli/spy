How to Play Scrabble

Objective: Score the most points by forming valid Azerbaijani words horizontally or vertically on the board.

First Turn: The first word played must cover the center star tile.

Connecting Words: All subsequent words must connect to at least one existing tile on the board.

Scoring: Each letter has a point value. The total score for a turn is the sum of the letters in all new words formed. Premium squares multiply the letter or word score:

  • 2x Letter (L2) / 3x Letter (L3)
  • 2x Word (W2) / 3x Word (W3)

Bingo: If you play all 7 tiles from your rack in one turn, you receive a 50-point bonus!

Special Tiles & Mechanics:

  • Blank Tile (*): Worth 0 points, but can represent any letter. You will be prompted to choose its letter when placed.
  • 🔄 Swap: Skip your turn to exchange any number of your tiles with the bag.
  • 🔀 Shuffle: Reorganize your rack (once per turn) to help spot words. This does NOT skip your turn.

How to Play Azul

Goal: Draft colored tiles to complete pattern lines, move them onto your wall to build your mosaic, and score points!

1. Drafting Phase (Factory Offer):

On your turn, you must take all tiles of one color from either:

  • Any Factory circle: The remaining tiles are pushed to the Center.
  • The Center of the table: If you are the first to do so this round, you also take the Starting Player tile 🪙 (placed on your floor line, gives -1 point but makes you start the next round).

Place all drafted tiles onto one of your 5 Pattern Lines. Any excess tiles that do not fit must be placed on your Floor Line.

Rule: A pattern line can only hold tiles of the same color, and you cannot place a color in a row if that color is already on the corresponding row of your wall.

2. Scoring Phase (Wall-Tiling):

After all tiles are drafted, players simultaneously tile their walls. For each completed pattern line:

  • Move 1 tile to the matching space on the wall row. Discard the rest of the line.
  • Scoring: Get 1 point for the tile. If there are adjacent tiles connected horizontally or vertically, get 1 point for each tile in those contiguous lines (e.g. a vertical row of 3 and horizontal row of 2 = 5 points).

Deduct penalties from tiles on the Floor Line (summing to -1, -2, -4, -6, -8, -11, -14 points). Then clear the floor.

3. Game End & End-Game Scoring:

The game ends after a Wall-Tiling phase where at least one player has completed a horizontal row of 5 tiles on their wall. Complete horizontal rows trigger the end of the game, after which final bonuses are tallied.

Add end-game bonuses:

  • +2 for each completed horizontal wall row.
  • +7 for each completed vertical wall column.
  • +10 for each color (all 5 tiles of that color are on the wall).
